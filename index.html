China's growth in numbers

Population Economy Military

Interactive visualization (slide show) showing some key indicators of China's rise and providing a context with 2 other countries - USSR/Russia and the United States which were the pre-eminent super-powers during the Cold War, with the US being able to be the sole super-power immediately after the collapse of the USSR.

It presents the data for 3 major indicators determining the "hard power" of a country. The user can select the data by the navigation bar.
